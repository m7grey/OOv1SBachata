What we do

Here at Sensual Movement Studio, Dance is a lifestyle. When you step into the studio, you enter a unique world of dance. The elegant ballroom transports you to a place all its own! As a lifestyle member, you experience more than just private lessons and group dance classes.

Group Classes

As a lifestyle member, you experience more than just private lessons and group dance classes.
NO PARTNER NEEDED, JUST HAVE FUN.

Workshops

You experience dance competitions, student showcases, workshops with instructors from all over the world, refreshment bar and more!

Social Parties

You experience dance practice parties with Djs and other members of the Sensual Movement Studio lifestyle, a place to share your dance lifestyle with friends and community.
